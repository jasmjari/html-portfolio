The Best Movies According to Jeanette

My top 3 movies of all time.

Selena

One of my all time favorite artist. I love the way the story of her short life was told.

Raising Victor Vargas

This movie shows what it is like to grow up being Dominican in NYC. Brings back many memories of my childhood.

Enough

Amazing movie that show the reality that many women face when it comes to domestic violence. The victim gets their happy ending.
